Our Mission

"Be A Buddhist"'s mission is to give people the platform to build routines and schedules to do their buddhist religious prayer. We hope that people will use our product to stay devoted with Buddhism and their own Spiritual journey, to discover what are the ways of a Buddhist, gain access to numerous free audio and mantra sound files, and to create playlist of them to organize personal prayer routines.
